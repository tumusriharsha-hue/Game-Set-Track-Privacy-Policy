Privacy Policy for GameSetTrack

Last Updated: June 2026

GameSetTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information GameSetTrack collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Profile photo (if provided)
  • Authentication provider information (Google Sign-In or email/password)
  • Anonymous guest account identifier (if using Guest Mode)

Match and Performance Data

GameSetTrack stores information that you enter while using the app, including:

  • UTR ratings
  • Match configurations and scoring formats
  • Points won and lost
  • Shot types and statistics
  • Performance trends
  • Personal match notes
  • Coach conversations and AI-generated analysis

Device Information

We may receive limited technical information necessary for authentication and synchronization, including:

  • Device identifiers generated by Firebase
  • App version information
  • Error logs and diagnostic information

How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

  • Provide match tracking features.
  • Authenticate user accounts.
  • Synchronize data between iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Generate AI coaching responses and performance analysis.
  • Improve reliability and performance.
  • Prevent abuse and secure our services.

AI Processing

GameSetTrack provides AI coaching features, including post-match analysis, trend analysis, and conversational coaching. To generate these responses, selected match statistics and user notes may be securely transmitted to our backend services and processed by Google's Gemini AI models.

We do not use your data to train public AI models.

Cloud Services

GameSetTrack uses:

  • Firebase Authentication for account management.
  • Supabase for secure cloud synchronization and backend services.
  • Google Gemini APIs for AI coaching features.

These providers process information only to provide the functionality of GameSetTrack.

Local Storage

Match data may be stored locally on your iPhone or Apple Watch using Apple's storage frameworks. Offline functionality is supported whenever possible.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We do not share personal information with advertisers.

Information is shared only with trusted service providers necessary to operate GameSetTrack, including Firebase, Supabase, and Google Gemini.

Guest Mode

Guest accounts are supported. Guest data remains associated with the anonymous account created on your device unless you choose to upgrade to a permanent account.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. Authentication is secured through Firebase Authentication, and AI requests are routed through secure backend services. No sensitive API keys are stored inside the app.

Children's Privacy

GameSetTrack is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

We retain your account and match information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your data.
  • Delete your account.
  • Request deletion of stored information.
  • Stop using cloud synchronization.
  • Use GameSetTrack in guest mode.

Third-Party Services

GameSetTrack relies on third-party providers whose privacy policies may also apply:

  • Google Firebase
  • Supabase
  • Google Gemini
  • Apple

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
